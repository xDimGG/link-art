FAQ
What is permanent jewelry? Permanet jewelry is a custom fit chain for your wrist, ankle, necklace, or a ring with a painless quick welding process. Because the jewelry is welded on, it does not have a clasp.
Does the welding process hurt? Nope. It is completely painless.
What if I need to take it off? If you need or want to remove your permanent jewelry, they can easily be cut at home with a pair of scissors. We can zap you back any time you want again.
Can you take your permanent jewelry through airport security? Yes. TSA does not ask you to remove fine jewelry when going through security.
Can my kid get one? We are happy to apply chains to kids with parental consent.
Can I get a return on permanent jewelry? Sadly, we cannot accept any returns on permanent jewelry pieces since they are custom cut just for you.
How long does the process take? After you select your chain, you can expect the process to take about 15 minutes.
